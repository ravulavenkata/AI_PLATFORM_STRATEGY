Risks & Mitigation

Data Privacy & Security

Mitigation: End-to-end encryption, Role-Based Access Control (RBAC), and implementing a secure-by-design architecture from day one.

Vendor Lock-In

Mitigation: Prioritize a multi-cloud compatible architecture using open-source standards (like Apache Iceberg and Spark) where possible to ensure portability.

Change Management & Adoption

Mitigation: Establish a formal Center of Excellence (CoE), develop a robust training program, and engage a network of internal champions early.
